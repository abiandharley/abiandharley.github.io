What's the venue like?

Bradbury Glade is basically a big tipi in the woods.

It's a semi-permanent structure, wheelchair accessible and with wooden floors throughout, so you won't have to bring your walking boots.

We'll be getting married in the ceremony "structure" (shed) which is just across from the tipi.

The tipi is fully enclosed and heated (including fire pits) so you won't be cold, but it will be April in the North East of England...!

Sorry, but guests are by invite only too. If they're not named on the invite, then we won't be able to accommodate them.

Is there a dress code?

It's a wedding, so preferably something at least semi-formal. The only big no-no is wearing a white dress!

Are you going for a triple-barrelled surname then?

Can you imagine us trying to fill out forms in the future if we were Mr & Mrs Robertson-Stevenson-Cocks?!

We're going for the modern approach and merging surnames, so we'll be Mr & Mrs Robertson-Cocks.

Do you have a wedding gift list?

We've been together for nearly 10 years so there's nothing we need. If you'd like to gift something, a contribution towards our honeymoon would be more than welcome.
